iOS & Android App

About the App

The GREENGAGE App is the official mobile front-end for the EU project GREENGAGE's Citizen Observatories. It enables citizen observers to conduct measurements, report observations, and participate in data collection activities within designated observatories.

The app is configured and managed through the GREENGAGE Console backend system at console.greengage.dev.

Key Features

  • Citizen Observatory Participation - Join and participate in environmental monitoring campaigns
  • Location-based Data Collection - Report observations at specific locations
  • Mobility Tracking - Track your journeys with automatic transport mode detection
  • Survey Completion - Answer structured questionnaires for research
  • Photo Documentation - Capture and upload images with your observations
  • Multi-Observatory Support - Switch between different Citizen Observatories
  • Gamification - Earn points and badges for contributions

App Screenshots

Welcome Screen

The welcome screen introduces the GREENGAGE mission: strengthening urban governance through citizen-based co-creation and crowdsourcing. Tap "Get Started" to begin the onboarding process.

Login & Registration

Secure authentication with multiple options:

  • Email & Password - Create a dedicated GREENGAGE account
  • Apple Sign-In - Quick login with your Apple ID (iOS)
  • Google Sign-In - Quick login with your Google account
  • Registration - Create a new account in seconds

Multi-language support allows switching between English and German.

Interactive Map

The main map interface displays your current Citizen Observatory with:

  • POIs (Points of Interest) - Predefined locations with tasks
  • Spots - User-contributed observations
  • Your Location - Real-time GPS positioning
  • Quick Actions:
    • "Add Spot" - Report a new observation
    • "Track me" - Start mobility tracking

Theme Filtering

Filter map content by themes defined by your observatory:

  • TRAFFIC - Traffic and mobility observations
  • CLIMATE - Environmental and climate data
  • Custom Themes - Any theme your observatory needs

Themes are fully customizable. Each Observatory can freely define its own themes through the GREENGAGE Console. This allows tailoring the app experience to your specific citizen science project needs.

Task Details & Surveys

Tasks provide clear instructions for participation:

  • Step-by-step guidance
  • Check-in functionality at locations
  • Integrated surveys with various question types
  • Point rewards for completion

Survey types include:

  • Multiple choice questions
  • Yes/No questions
  • Rating scales
  • Free text input

Add Spot

Citizens can report observations by:

  1. Selecting a theme category
  2. Adding a title for the observation
  3. Writing a description
  4. Uploading a photo for documentation

Spots are immediately visible to other users and administrators.

Profile & Settings

Manage your account:

  • View and edit profile information
  • Switch Observatories - Participate in multiple citizen science projects
  • Change language settings
  • Sign out or restart onboarding
  • View app version (current: v2.2.1)

Privacy & Data

The app collects the following data for functionality:

Data Type Purpose Linked to Identity
Location (precise & coarse) Observatory participation Yes
Photos/Videos Spot documentation Yes
Contact Info Account management Yes
Device Identifiers Analytics No
Crash Data Bug fixing No

All data collection follows GDPR guidelines.
